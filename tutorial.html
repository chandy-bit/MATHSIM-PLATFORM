Math Cheat Sheet

Slope formula: m = (y₂ - y₁)/(x₂ - x₁)
Slope-intercept: y = mx + b
Quadratic: y = ax² + bx + c
Vertex x: -b/(2a)
Discriminant: b² - 4ac
Parallel lines: same slope
Perpendicular: m₁ × m₂ = -1
Distance formula: √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]
