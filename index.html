Learn Anywhere - Offline Mode

MathSim works completely offline! Here's how:

Install the App

Click "Install MathSim App" button when prompted, or look for the install icon in your browser address bar.

Automatic Caching

Once you visit a page, it's saved for offline use. All simulations, games, and tutorials work without internet!

Progress Syncs

Your learning progress saves locally and syncs automatically when you're back online.

🔌

No Internet Required

After initial load, you can use MathSim on planes, during commutes, or anywhere without WiFi!

How to Install:

  1. Desktop: Look for the install icon (📲) in the address bar or click "Install MathSim App" button
  2. Mobile (iOS): Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Mobile (Android): Tap Menu → "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"
