MathSim User Manual

Getting Started

Welcome to MathSim! This guide will help you navigate the platform and make the most of your learning experience.

1. Choose Your Subject

Browse through our subject menu to select the area you want to study: Algebra, Geometry, Pre-Calculus, Calculus, or Probability.

2. Interactive Simulations

Adjust sliders to see how changing parameters affects graphs in real-time. Use the equation display to see the mathematical representation.

3. Play Games

Test your knowledge with our Slope Challenge game. Answer correctly to earn points and level up!

4. Track Progress

Visit the Progress page to see your learning analytics, including tutorials watched, games played, and accuracy percentages.

5. Accessibility

Customize your experience with high contrast mode, large text, reduced motion, and dyslexic-friendly font options.

FAQ

Is MathSim free?

Yes! MathSim is completely free and open source. No account required.

Does it work offline?

Yes! After your first visit, you can use MathSim offline. Look for the install button to add it to your home screen.

How do I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser. You can also export your data from the Progress page.
