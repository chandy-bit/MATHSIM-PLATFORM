Pre-Calculus Cheat Sheet

sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
sin(2θ) = 2 sinθ cosθ
cos(2θ) = cos²θ - sin²θ
lim(x→0) sin x / x = 1
Arithmetic Series: S = n(a₁ + aₙ)/2
Geometric Series: S = a₁/(1-r)
Vector Magnitude: |v| = √(x² + y²)
Dot Product: u·v = |u||v|cosθ
Polar to Cartesian: x = r cosθ, y = r sinθ
Cartesian to Polar: r = √(x²+y²), θ = tan⁻¹(y/x)
