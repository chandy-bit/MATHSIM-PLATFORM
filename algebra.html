Algebra Cheat Sheet

Slope formula: m = (y₂ - y₁)/(x₂ - x₁)
Slope-intercept: y = mx + b
Quadratic formula: x = [-b ± √(b² - 4ac)] / 2a
Discriminant: Δ = b² - 4ac
Factoring (a=1): x² + (p+q)x + pq = (x+p)(x+q)
Log rule: logₐ(b) = ln(b)/ln(a)
Exponent rule: aᵐ × aⁿ = aᵐ⁺ⁿ
Vertex formula: x = -b/(2a)
