Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MathSim ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our educational platform. MathSim is designed with privacy-first principles - all your learning data stays on your device unless you choose to export it.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide:

  • Name - Optional, used only for personalization on your device
  • Learning preferences - Accessibility settings, language choices, and theme preferences
  • Progress data - Tutorials watched, games played, scores, and achievements

2.2 Automatically collected information (stored locally only):

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, desktop)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your learning experience
  • To track your progress and achievements
  • To improve our educational content
  • To provide technical support
  • To remember your accessibility preferences

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not store any personal information on external servers. Your learning progress never leaves your device unless you:

  • Explicitly export your data using the "Export Data" button
  • Manually share your saved data
Privacy First - Unlike many online learning platforms, MathSim does not have any backend servers. Your data belongs to you and stays with you.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • YouTube - For tutorial videos. YouTube may collect data according to their own privacy policy. We do not control or have access to this data.
  • Chart.js - For analytics visualizations. All data stays local.

6. Children's Privacy

MathSim is designed for learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. All data remains on the user's device and can be cleared at any time using the "Reset Progress" button.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to review their children's progress directly within the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data - View on the Progress page
  • Delete your data - Use "Reset All Progress" button
  • Export your data - Use "Export Data" button on Progress page
  • Opt out - Disable optional features in Accessibility settings

8. Data Security

Since all data stays on your device, security is managed by your browser and operating system. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using a secure device
  • Exporting important progress data as backup

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of MathSim after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@mathsim.org
  • Website: mathsim.org
  • Via accessibility feedback form

MathSim is committed to protecting your privacy. No data is sold, shared, or transmitted to external servers, and all data is anonymised.
