Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MathSim. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

What is MathSim? ▼

MathSim is an interactive mathematics learning platform that combines simulations, games, tutorials, and learning analytics to help students understand mathematical concepts through visual exploration and practice. The platform covers Algebra, Geometry, Pre-Calculus, Calculus, and Probability.

Do I need to create an account? ▼

No! MathSim does not require an account. All your progress is saved locally on your device using your browser's storage. You can start using the platform immediately without any sign-up process.

Is MathSim free? ▼

Yes! MathSim is completely free to use. No subscription, no hidden fees, no ads. The platform is open source and maintained for educational purposes.

How do I use the interactive simulations? ▼

Using simulations is simple:

  1. Go to the Simulations page
  2. Select the function type (linear, quadratic, cubic, etc.)
  3. Adjust parameters using the sliders
  4. Watch the graph update in real-time
  5. Use preset buttons to see common configurations
What types of functions can I graph? ▼

MathSim supports multiple function types:

  • Linear: y = mx + b
  • Quadratic: y = ax² + bx + c
  • Cubic: y = ax³ + bx² + cx + d
  • Trigonometric: sin(x), cos(x), tan(x)
  • Exponential: y = a·bˣ
Can I save my graphs? ▼

Yes! Use Ctrl + S to save your current graph settings, and Ctrl + L to load them later. You can also export your graph as an image using the export button.

How do I play the Slope Challenge game? ▼

In the Slope Challenge game, you'll see a line on a coordinate grid. Your task is to identify its slope from the given options. Each correct answer earns points, and consecutive correct answers increase your score multiplier. You have three lives - use them wisely!

What happens when I get an answer wrong? ▼

When you answer incorrectly:

  • You lose one life (hearts)
  • The correct answer is highlighted to help you learn
  • Your streak resets to zero
  • You can use the hint button for help on the next question
How does the scoring system work? ▼

Score is calculated as follows:

  • Base points: 10 × current level
  • Streak bonus: Each correct answer adds to your streak multiplier
  • Higher levels = more points per correct answer
  • Your best score is saved and displayed on the progress page
What accessibility features does MathSim offer? ▼

MathSim is committed to accessibility:

  • High Contrast Mode: Improved visibility for users with visual impairments
  • Large Text Mode: Increased font size throughout the platform
  • Reduced Motion: Disables animations for users sensitive to motion
  • Dyslexic Font: OpenDyslexic font option for easier reading
  • Screen Reader Support: Full ARIA labels and semantic HTML
  • Keyboard Navigation: Complete functionality without a mouse

Visit the Accessibility page to customize your experience.

Does MathSim work with screen readers? ▼

Yes! MathSim is designed to work with screen readers like NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver. All interactive elements have descriptive ARIA labels, and dynamic content updates are announced to screen readers. The graph descriptions provide text alternatives for visual content.

Does MathSim work offline? ▼

Yes! MathSim is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once you visit the site, it caches all necessary files for offline use. You can access simulations, games, and tutorials even without an internet connection. Your progress saves locally and syncs when you reconnect.

How do I install MathSim as an app? ▼

Installing MathSim is easy:

  • Desktop (Chrome/Edge): Click the install icon in the address bar or click the "Install MathSim App" button
  • Mobile (iOS): Tap Share → "Add to Home Screen"
  • Mobile (Android): Tap Menu → "Install App" or "Add to Home Screen"
What browsers are supported? ▼

MathSim works on all modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (recommended)
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari (macOS and iOS)
  • Microsoft Edge

For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of Chrome or Firefox.

How is my data stored and protected? ▼

Your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not collect or store any personal information on external servers. You have complete control over your data and can export or delete it at any time from the Progress page. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I track my learning progress? ▼

Absolutely! The Progress page tracks:

  • Tutorials watched
  • Games played
  • Accuracy percentage
  • Best scores
  • Total study time
  • Achievements unlocked

You can also export your progress data for backup or analysis.

How do I change the language? ▼

To change the language:

  1. Look for the language selector in the top-right corner of the page
  2. Click the dropdown menu
  3. Select your preferred language (English, Español, Français, Deutsch)
  4. The page content will update immediately

Your language preference is saved for future visits.
